News

The Ringing Room is currently under very active development; features may change or be added without warning! Works best on a laptop or desktop with Google Chrome. Microsoft Edge does NOT work for the time being; use other browser if possible.

Recent changes:
  • 11 April 2020: Continued iteration on username implementation.
  • 9 April 2020: Fixed many bugs with usernames, and with strokes getting out of sync between users; updated handbell audio.
  • 5 April 2020: Added (transient) usernames and bell-assignment capabilities. Redesigned tower layout.
  • 1 April 2020: Issues with UK keyboard layout fixed. List of known bugs and available fixes added to Help page.
  • 31 March 2020: Tower IDs (and links to specific towers) should now be stable indefinitely.
  • 30 March 2020: Added an option to use audio from handbells rather than towerbells, on 6 or 8. (Not currently available on 10 or 12.)
