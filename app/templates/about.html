The site

Welcome to the Ringing Room! This is a site built for change ringers to continue ringing with one another even when socially distanced. It is under development currently, and new features may show up with little warning. Please contact us if you notice any bugs.

The developers

The Ringing Room is a joint venture by Leland Paul Kusmer and Bryn Marie Reinstadler, both ringers in the Boston, Massachusetts area.

The audio

The tower bell audio is provided by Bill Hibbert. The handbell recordings are of Leland's bells, with editing and extension by JES.

Related Efforts

Richard Johnston has done a great deal of work to make Ringing Room work with Handbell Manager and with Android phones as simulated handbells (!). Read more about it by clicking here.
